## Directions

1. Mix oatmeal with milk, yoghurt and raisins
2. Grate lemon peel and squeze lemon juice into the mix
3. Grate (or cut) apples and other fruits into the mix
4. Add hazelnuts and cream and stir well
6. Serve in small bowl

## Original 1900's Recipe

This dish was invented by swiss doctor Maximilian Oskar Bircher-Benner in the early 1900's.
His original recipe for a single serving contained the following:

- 1 tbsp of oatmeal
- 3 tbsp of water
- 1 tbsp of lemon juice
- 1 tbsp of sweetend condensed milk
- 2 grated apples
- 1 tbsp of hazelnuts or almonds
